myGate: Apartment Security and Visitor Management System

Security management systems in apartments and gated communities have always been based on primitive pen and paper system. Security guards spend most of their time in communicating visitor details with the residents. Even after all this there is no visibility into the movement of visitors inside and outside the community. There is so much information about apartment service staff at the main gate, yet residents are anxious every morning about their household help. myGate solves all these security and visitor problems with an intelligent app based solution.

myGate is an Intelligent Security Management for Gated Premises which automates and digitizes manual tasks at the main gate(s). Apartments can deploy myGate in less than a week without any investment in hardware or any capital expenditure. We provide the solution in a pay-as-you-go pricing model. When we deploy myGate Apartment Security and Visitor Management System, we take care of the training of community management committee, facility staff and security guards. And all of this at a negligible cost! myGate is installed in all the popular and premium properties in Bengaluru including Mantri Espana, Adarsh Palm Retreat, Purva Riviera, Prestige Ozone etc.

myGate security management system can be deployed in a multi-tiered system where there are multiple security check points, viz. at the main gate and at the building gate. Our solution also helps you manage visitor car parking easily. Finding out the owner of a randomly parked vehicle inside the community is always a hassle. myGate app will connect you directly with the owner of the vehicle in such cases. The app is easy to use and some necessary functions of this app can be used even if the residents do not have a mobile or a smartphone. We have recently introduced the child safety and security feature as well. Parents can now approve or deny their children’s movement outside the community in their absence. The security system is designed in such a way that it is hassle free for both senior citizens and children.
